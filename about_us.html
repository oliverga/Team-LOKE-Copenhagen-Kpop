About us

We are mother and daughter, with the vision that music creates meaning and brings people together. We want to offer a cozy Kpop business in a space of excitement and therefore opened Copenhagen Kpop during the Covid shutdown in the summer of 2021, where we stumbled upon the absolutely perfect premises in the atmospheric Latin quarter in the heart of Copenhagen.

A Welcoming Universe

We wanted a Kpop store that would shine with color and joy. A cozy shop with presence and desire to listen to music together - A space where you could have a chat with each other, meet new fans and find your next inspiration.

We are grateful for our first year in Copenhagen Kpop with visiting customers from all over the world. And we are excited to present a webshop that, like our store, has been set up with simplicity so you can find your favorite album in an easy way.
